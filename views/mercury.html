Welcome to Mercury!

Mercury, the planet closest to the sun. As such it is a lot hotter there and so temperatures can reach up to 840 degrees Fahrenheit. The name Mercury comes a God in Roman beliefs which was described as "fleet-footed" since the planet revolves around the Sun the fastest. To put it into perspective Mercury is known as the smallest planet, only slightly larger than Earth's moon.
